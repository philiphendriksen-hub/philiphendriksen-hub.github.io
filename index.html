About OVERINFLATED — Best Simple Mobile Game 2026

OVERINFLATED is a free hyper-casual balloon arcade game built by solo indie developer Philip Hendriksen. The balloon inflates on its own — your only job is to tap and vent air to stay the right size. Thread through gaps, dodge obstacles, and don't pop. With one-tap controls, zero ads, zero paywalls, instant retry, 10 unlockable skins, and rising difficulty, OVERINFLATED is one of the best simple mobile games of 2026. If you liked Flappy Bird, you'll love this.

🎈

One-Tap Mechanic

Tap to vent air and shrink. Stop tapping and the balloon inflates. Simple to learn, brutal to master.

How It Works

Three things will kill you.

01

Pop — You're too big

The balloon inflates on its own. If you don't vent in time, it'll outgrow the gap and hit the pins.

02

Drift — You're too small

Vent too much and the balloon shrinks. It loses momentum, drifts sideways, and clips the wall.

03

Squeeze — Wrong size, wrong gap

Every obstacle has a different gap width. You need the perfect size at the perfect time.
